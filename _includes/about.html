About

Google Developer Group Birgunj [GDG Birgunj] is a group of developers, Google technology enthusiasts, and Googlers from around mid-terai region, Nepal. We discuss, share our ideas, experiences and plan for events, meetups near.

  • 2013

    Our Humble Beginnings

    "Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic" Keeping this in our mind we started with only 2 member, Dipesh Kumar & Nitesh Chaurasiya. Google I/O Extended at Kathmandu & Birgunj were one of our first event.
